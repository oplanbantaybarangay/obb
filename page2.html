Thoughts on
"Bulacan grade school teacher arrested, accused of raping student"

A grade school teacher from Bocaue, Bulacan has been arrested after one of his students accused him of raping her for more than a year.

The world is not safe anymore; that is a fact. A fact that everyone knows but still, we can't do anything about it. We can never do anything to stop the crimes and bad doings in the world in just a snap.. We cannot control world peace and safety for it requires everyone to strictly cooperate. Of course, some would and many would not and that is the entire reason why the world is not safe. Yes, there are still a lot of good hearts out there but who really knows the exact percentage of the bad people who will harm you everytime they get a chance? Nobody ever does. The fact that the world is not safe is in its permanent position - it cannot be changed. Nevertheless, we can always do something. Like a business agreement, we can always negotiate. It is true that anytime might be your last second in this world but maybe, the best preparation is to be alert. Indeed, prevention is better than cure.

In the past months, it has been a struggle for every citizen of this country to ensure their safety and security due to the unstoppable increasing crime rate that results to their craving for awareness. It is not a surprise that everytime news reports are being flashed in the television or even in the social media network sites, a spine-chilling crime have become its main dish for the night. Even a school zone is not exempted when it comes to the list of many crime scenes and that's why crimes are now being described along the lines terrifying, scandalous, merciless and horrendous.

A girl was found helplessly raped and molested by her own teacher. That news became the talk of the town for a few weeks. Isn't it ironic that the school should be a safe place for its students for it is their second home and they spend half of their lives there yet stuffs like these still happen. It is a good thing that the victim had the courage to speak up and inform the authorities of her dreadful situation. It must be so hard to report the series of harassments she got for it is so humiliating and self-degrading in her part. Admitting that you got raped must have attracted many judgmental people who listens but only knows how to jump into conclusions that are pleasant to their ears. Thus results to unwanted psychological problems of the victims. Aside from trauma, the victim can get depressed and might have a lot of suicidal thoughts that potencially can ruin not only her socializing skills but her entire life.

That is just an instance. One among many that is really happening in this world. This world is a mixture of both chaos and danger but we can always live in peace if we really want to. Horrendous crimes happen everytime we breathe and we really can't do anything about it but to be prepared. Of course, we can't always be wary of the bad things (we all hope not) that can happen to us, maybe the best thing we can do is to just enjoy life and love your loved ones as if it's your last day on earth and of course, be safe. Yeah, YOLO. That way, you can live a life complete with love and happiness sans regrets while staying safe and alert.
